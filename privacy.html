Privacy policy

Short version: IgniteX collects almost nothing. Your Steam username and password go directly to Valve and are never seen by us. The app has no analytics and no tracking. The only personal data we handle is what's needed to sell you a licence and let you activate it.

Who we are

The data controller is IgniteX, operated by [DOPLNIT: jméno / název, IČO, adresa], Czech Republic. For any privacy question or request, contact support@ignitex.app.

What we collect, and what we don't

Purchase (handled by Lemon Squeezy)

Payments are processed by Lemon Squeezy as Merchant of Record. They collect your email and payment details to complete the sale, send your receipt and licence key, and meet tax obligations. We receive your email address and order/licence information from them, which we use to deliver the product and provide support. See Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy for how they process payment data; IgniteX never receives your full card number.

Licence activation

To confirm your purchase and prevent unlimited copying, the app validates your licence key with Lemon Squeezy's licence service and stores the key plus a locally-generated device identifier in your Mac's Keychain. The device identifier is used only to count how many machines a licence is active on. We do not build profiles and do not use this for advertising.

Steam login — never touches us

When you sign in to Steam inside IgniteX, you are talking to Valve's servers through the official Steam client running on your Mac. Your Steam credentials and Steam Guard codes are never transmitted to, stored by, or visible to IgniteX.

No analytics or tracking

The IgniteX app contains no telemetry, no crash-reporting SDKs, and no third-party analytics. This website uses no third-party advertising or tracking cookies. Diagnostic logs the app writes stay on your Mac (in ~/Library/Application Support/SteamBridge/Logs) and are never uploaded unless you choose to send one to support.

Legal basis & retention

We process your email and order data to perform our contract with you (delivering the licence and support) and to comply with tax/accounting law. We keep order records for as long as legally required, and support correspondence for as long as needed to help you. Licence/device data is kept while your licence is active.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, to object to or restrict processing, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in the Czech Republic, the Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů). To exercise any of these, email support@ignitex.app. Note that some records (e.g. invoices) must be retained to meet legal obligations even after a deletion request.
